Shemai
very tasty dessert
http://goldentiffin.co/blog/2016/8/4/best-of-bengali-shemai-sweet-vermicelli-pudding
Ingredients

100g vermicelli, broken into pieces for roasting 

1 tbsp ghee 

pinch of saffron  

1 bay leaf  

1 cinnamon stick  

2-3 cardamom pods, crushed 

1 star anise

300ml whole milk (plus extra if needed)

200ml single cream 

3-4 tbsp sugar
