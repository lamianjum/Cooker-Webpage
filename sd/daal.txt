Masoor Dal Recipe

Here is a very easy to make massor dal recipe. Indian red lentil recipe has the tadka of garlic and tomatoes which makes it very aromatic. Learn how to make masoor dal

http://www.indianfoodforever.com/daal/masoor-dal.html

Ingredients:

1 cup whole masoor dal
1 tsp garam masala
1 tsp turmeric
1 tsp salt
2 - 3 medium tomatoes
1/3 cup oil
1 onion, chopped
4 - 5 pods garlic, separated, chopped finely
1 inch ginger root, chopped
2 green chillies, chopped
1 bunch coriander, chopped
Water
