Daal puri
Daal Puri is an easy recipe which is made by stuffing boiled potatoes with spices
https://recipes.timesofindia.com/recipes/dal-puri/rs55051886.cms

Ingredients
500 gm all purpose flour
1 teaspoon red chilli
1 teaspoon garam masala powder
2 cloves garlic
2 small green chilli
100 gm moong dal
1 teaspoon coriander powder
4 teaspoon refined oil
1 medium chopped onion
2 teaspoon salt
