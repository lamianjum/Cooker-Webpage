 Mutton Korma
Here is an authentic mutton korma recipe which is very easy to follow.
http://www.indianfoodforever.com/non-veg/mutton/mutton-korma.html

Ingredients:

750 gms Mutton
3/4 cup Fried Onions (store bought / home made)
1 1/2 tblsp Coriander Powder
1 1/2 tsp Red Chilly Powder (For heat)
1 1/2 tblsp Kashmiri Red Chilly Powder (For Color)
2 tblsp Garlic Paste
2 tblsp Ginger Paste
8 tblsp Curd
3 tblsp Ghee / Oil
2 Cups water
Few Kewra Essence Drop
Salt To Taste
Whole Spices:
2 Bay Leaf
2 Black Cardamom
5 - 6 Green Cardamom
7 Cloves
2" Cinnamom Stick
For Garnish
1 tblsp Ginger Juliennes
