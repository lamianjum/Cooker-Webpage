Ingredients for Egg chop Recipe
Eggs – 3 (boiled)
Potatoes – 6 (boiled)
Jeera (cumin)powder –  3 tsp
Chilli powder – 1 tsp
Turmeric powder – ½ tsp
Ginger-garlic paste – 2 tsp
Salt – to taste
Pepper– to taste
Coriander leaves – 1 tbsp(chopped) (optional)
Bread crumbs – to coat the chop
Egg – 1 (beaten ) to coat the chop
Oil – for frying
