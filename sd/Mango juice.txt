Aamras Recipe(Mango juice)

Aamras is the puree or plup of the mango. People enjoy is by putting some flavours like Rose syrup or Cardamom or almonds, saffron etc in it for getting that flavoured twist.

http://www.indianfoodforever.com/bachelor-cooking/aamras.html

Ingredients:

1 kg Mango (Aam)
200 gms powdered Sugar (Cheeni)
1/2 litre Milk (Doodh)
